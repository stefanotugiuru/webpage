Filming Across Europe: Lessons Learned

Nov 28, 2024 6 min read Travel
di Stefano Maurici

My recent travels through Europe taught me invaluable lessons about adapting to different cultures and locations. From navigating train systems in Italy to filming street performances in France, I learned how to combine planning with spontaneity for the best shots.

I realized that flexibility is key: schedules can change, weather can be unpredictable, and equipment can fail. By embracing these challenges, I discovered new creative possibilities and unique stories that would otherwise be missed.

Collaboration is another essential lesson. Working with local guides, fellow travelers, and even strangers opened doors to experiences I could never have anticipated. Each interaction added depth and authenticity to my storytelling.

Finally, patience and observation became my greatest tools. Taking the time to really see the environment, understand the people, and anticipate moments allowed me to capture shots that were both visually compelling and emotionally resonant.
