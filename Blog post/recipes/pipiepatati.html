Pipi e Patati

Dec 17, 2025 5 min read Recipes
by Stefano Maurici

Watch the recipe video

Some dishes tell the story of a place better than words ever could. Pipi and Potatoes is one of them: a simple, rustic recipe straight from the heart of Calabria.

This dish is all about balance. Spicy peppers, golden potatoes and good olive oil come together to create something honest and deeply comforting. No shortcuts, no fancy techniques — just real ingredients and time.

Ingredients

  • Fresh peppers (preferably spicy)
  • Medium potatoes
  • Onion (optional)
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt, to taste
  • Black pepper, to taste
  • Chili pepper
  • (Optional) Fresh basil

Method

  1. Wash the peppers, remove the ends and slice them into rings or strips.
  2. Peel the potatoes and cut them into thin slices or small cubes.
  3. Heat olive oil in a pan and cook potatoes until golden.
  4. Add peppers, cover and cook gently.
  5. Cook until soft and nicely browned.
  6. Add onion if desired.
  7. Season to taste.
  8. Serve hot with basil.

A personal tip

This recipe works best when you don’t rush it. Low heat, a wide pan and a bit of patience make all the difference. Perfect as a side dish, but also great as a rustic appetizer to share.
