About Me

Hi, I’m Stefano, I am a communicator, a storyteller, a marketer, and a creator. I’ve always believed that stories shape the way we see the world. Mine started in a small village in Calabria, where curiosity was my way of exploring places I hadn’t yet seen. That same curiosity took me far, across countries, languages, and creative worlds, from classrooms where I studied international marketing to projects where ideas turned into campaigns, and strategies into stories. Over time, I’ve learned that marketing isn’t just about selling, it’s about connecting. It’s the art of listening, understanding people, and transforming insights into something that speaks to both the heart and the mind. Today, I work at the intersection of creativity and strategy, where intuition meets data, and where every project begins with a simple question, what story are we really trying to tell
